Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using SearchFind

General Questions

SearchFind is a comprehensive job board platform that connects employers with job seekers. We provide tools for employers to post job listings and for job seekers to find and apply for jobs that match their skills and interests.

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the page. You'll need to provide your email address and create a password. You can then choose whether you're a job seeker or an employer and complete your profile.

SearchFind offers both free and premium features. Basic job searching and applications are free for job seekers. Employers can post a limited number of job listings for free. Premium subscriptions are available for both job seekers and employers to access advanced features.

You can contact our customer support team by visiting the Contact Us page or by sending an email to support@searchfind.com. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.

For Job Seekers

You can search for jobs using the search bar on the homepage or by browsing job categories. You can filter results by location, job type, experience level, and more to find the most relevant opportunities.

To apply for a job, click on the job listing to view the details, then click the "Apply Now" button. You'll need to upload your resume and complete any additional application questions. Some jobs may redirect you to the employer's website to complete the application.

A Pro subscription for job seekers includes benefits such as:

  • AI-powered resume builder and analysis
  • Job match percentage to see how well you fit each position
  • Priority application status
  • Early access to new job listings
  • Interview preparation tools
  • Salary insights and negotiation tips

You can track all your job applications in your Job Seeker Dashboard. This shows the status of each application, any messages from employers, and allows you to withdraw applications if needed.

For Employers

To post a job, you need to first register your company. Once your company is approved, you can click on "Post a Job" in your Employer Dashboard and fill out the job details form. You can preview the listing before publishing it.

You can manage all applications through your Employer Dashboard. You can view applicant profiles, download resumes, change application statuses (pending, shortlisted, interview, etc.), and communicate with candidates directly through our messaging system.

Premium job listings offer several advantages:

  • Featured placement on the homepage and search results
  • Extended visibility period
  • AI-powered candidate matching
  • Advanced analytics on job performance
  • Branded company profile
  • Access to our database of qualified candidates

You can renew or extend a job listing from your Employer Dashboard. Navigate to the job you want to extend, click on "Renew Job," and select a renewal package. You can also extend the application deadline for an active job without purchasing a new package.

Subscriptions & Payments

We offer different subscription plans for both job seekers and employers. Job seekers can choose from Basic (free), Pro, and Premium plans. Employers can select from Starter, Business, and Enterprise plans. Each plan includes different features and benefits. You can view all available plans on our Subscription Plans page.

To upgrade your subscription, go to your account settings and select "Manage Subscription." From there, you can view available plans and select the one that best meets your needs. You'll be guided through the payment process to complete your upgrade.

We accept payments through Paystack, which supports various payment methods including credit/debit cards and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely, and we do not store your payment information.

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Go to your account settings, select "Manage Subscription," and click on "Cancel Subscription." Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period, after which it will not renew.

Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. If you're experiencing issues with your subscription or believe you've been charged incorrectly, please contact our support team at billing@searchfind.com within 7 days of the charge.
